# Roadmap Critique Template

## Self-Assess Your Product Roadmap in 30 Minutes

This template helps you identify gaps, misalignments, and opportunities in your product roadmap.

---

## Section 1: Strategic Alignment (Score: __/25)

### Does your roadmap support your product strategy?

- [ ] **Every roadmap item ties to strategic objective** (5 pts)
- [ ] **No "orphan features" (requested but not strategic)** (5 pts)
- [ ] **Resource allocation matches strategic priorities** (5 pts)
- [ ] **Clear connection between roadmap and company OKRs** (5 pts)
- [ ] **Team can explain WHY each item is on roadmap** (5 pts)

**Red flags:**
- Features from random customer requests
- "Nice to have" taking up >20% of capacity
- Roadmap doesn't reference strategy doc

---

## Section 2: Customer Value (Score: __/25)

### Does your roadmap deliver measurable customer value?

- [ ] **Each item has clear customer outcome defined** (5 pts)
- [ ] **Success metrics defined before building** (5 pts)
- [ ] **Based on research/data, not opinions** (5 pts)
- [ ] **Addresses top customer pain points** (5 pts)
- [ ] **Will customers pay for this / increase usage?** (5 pts)

**Test:** Can you explain value in one sentence per feature?

---

## Section 3: Sequencing & Dependencies (Score: __/20)

### Is your roadmap sequenced logically?

- [ ] **Dependencies identified and managed** (5 pts)
- [ ] **Riskiest assumptions tested first** (5 pts)
- [ ] **Quick wins balanced with long-term bets** (5 pts)
- [ ] **No impossible timelines** (5 pts)

**Red flag:** Everything marked "high priority"

---

## Section 4: Feasibility (Score: __/15)

### Can you actually build this?

- [ ] **Engineering capacity validated** (5 pts)
- [ ] **Technical feasibility confirmed** (5 pts)
- [ ] **Design resources allocated** (5 pts)

**Test:** Ask eng lead "Can we do this?" If they hesitate, you have a problem.

---

## Section 5: Communication (Score: __/15)

### Is your roadmap understood?

- [ ] **Stakeholders aligned on priorities** (5 pts)
- [ ] **Updated regularly (at least monthly)** (5 pts)
- [ ] **Accessible to entire org** (5 pts)

---

- **50-69:** Needs significant work
- **<50:** Start over
